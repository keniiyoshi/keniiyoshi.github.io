Genesis Embodied AI Logo Download Instructions
===============================================

To add the Genesis Embodied AI logo to this folder:

1. Visit: https://github.com/Genesis-Embodied-AI/Genesis
2. Navigate to the docs/assets directory
3. Look for the logo file (usually named logo.png or genesis_logo.png)
4. Download the logo file
5. Rename it to "genesis-logo.png" and place it in this img folder

Alternative method:
- Search for "Genesis Embodied AI logo" on Google Images
- Find the official logo
- Right-click and "Save image as..."
- Save as "genesis-logo.png" in this folder

Once downloaded, you can reference it in your HTML as:
<img src="img/genesis-logo.png" alt="Genesis Embodied AI Logo">
